About me

I am a researcher in the areas of computer architecture with particular interests in the intersection of high-performance computing (HPC) systems and deep learning. I received my Ph.D. in computer engineering from the University of Texas at Austin in 2014 (thesis advisor: Mattan Erez). I received my M.S. and B.S. degrees from KAIST (Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology) and Sogang University, in 2009 and 2007, respectively. My research experience spans several areas in computing including ASIC designs, computer system architecture, and application and workload characterizations with an emphasis on deep neural networks.
