About me

I am a computer architect working as a researcher at NVIDIA. My research focuses on realizing energy-efficient, high-performance memory and processor architectures that will power future supercomputers and artificially intelligent machines. I received my PhD in computer engineering from the University of Texas at Austin under the guidance of Mattan Erez in 2014.
